Admission for B.E. Substream EXTC

ADMISSION PROCESS In order to get admission into Thadomal Shahani Engineering College for the course of Electronics and Telecommunications, I first filled my application form on the official website of DTE http://www.dtemaharashtra.gov.in/index.html.
There are three stages in the online application form, namely
1. Filling up the personal and educational details
2. Document verification
3. Filling the college preference list (where you can input maximum 30 colleges, in the order of your preference)
The verification of all the documents is done online and we have to upload scanned copies of all the documents mentioned on their website.
I appeared for the entrance exams: JEE and MH-CET; although if you're a resident of Maharashtra you don't need to appear for JEE, MH-CET is necessary for all the in-state students. The Admission process started a week after the MH-cet entrance exam results were out. There were three lists released by the government, with a gap of 15-20 days. As the lists are released, the applicants are allotted a college based on their preference list and their merit rank (based on the entrance exam and the HSC grades combined). One can also apply via the offline mode by filling the offline admission form, based on the merit and cut offs, one can secure a seat through the offline mode.

SECURING YOUR ADMISSION SEAT
We can secure our admission by visiting the allotted college and submitting the hard copies of all the documents and a minimum reservation fees. I was allotted this college in the third list, hence I secured my seat by filling the college admission form of TSEC along with verifying and submitting the original documents and the payment of fees in the form of a Demand Draft of the total amount. I needed to visit the college twice for the same. After the college confirms your admission for the desired course, you receive a SMS by the official DTE website stating the confirmation of your admission.

LIST OF DOCUMENTS
All documents right from 10th standard and also some other as stated below:
Pass certificates of class X and XII
Marks Sheets of class X and XII
Proof of DOB (Class X certificate)
MH-CET/JEE admit cards
MH-CET/JEE score cards
Seat allotment letter
Proof of fee payment
Photographs as used in admit card
Income certificate if apply against TWFS/Fee Concession
Proper certificate if belong to reserved category
Blue and black pens
Pencil
Eraser
Glue
Staples
Whitener

SCHOLARSHIP AND ASSISTANCE
1. The students who belong to the reserved categories, can select the option of 'TFWS(Tution Fee Waiver Scheme)' while filling the online application form on the dte website. Depending on your merit and the course-college selected in your preference, you may receive this government scholarship.

This scholarship can be availed in any year of your engineering. In order to apply for this scholarship, your admission should be through the online procedure only.

AFTER THE ADMISSION
After completing all the legal procedures, we were given the roll numbers and directed to our classes. Also, we were connected to our classmates by a WhatsApp group, where we received all the regular updates for our lectures, timings, holidays, etc.
